Breakfast Scramble

Description

The breakfast scramble is an quick and easy meal to prepare for those with little time to get a nutrious breakfast in. It only takes about 15 minutes to prepare and can be made the night before for those who enjoy a meal on the go. It can even be catared to fit the right amount of protein in for those who obsess about eating the right amount of macros. In this page, I will walk you through exactly what you will need and how to make your own breakfast scramble.

List of Ingredients

  • Three Eggs. This amount can be altered but I find three to be the right amount.
  • Choice of Protein. I usually opt for turkey breast but bacon and other proteins can definetly be used.
  • A quarter of an onion.
  • A whole Potato. The type of potato is not important
  • Avocado or olive oil.
  • Cajun seasoning.
  • Green onions optional.

Steps

  1. Start off by dicing a whole potato into cube sized pieces. I usually cut it in half to begin with. Make sure your potato is properly washed.

  2. After dicing your potato, toss into a bowl of ice water in order to remove the starch so they don't stick to each other for 3 minutes. Drain the water and place the bowl into a microwave for five minutes on high.

  3. While the potatos are in the microwave, dice the onion into small pieces and set aside. Do the same with your choice of protein.

  4. Once your potatos are done. Heat a cast iron skillet with a 3 tablespoons of oil on medium high heat. Once hot enough, empty the bowl of potatos into the skillet making sure to separate any potatos stuck together. Let the potatos cook for 3 minutes.

  5. Once the potatos start to brown, toss in your chopped onion and your protein. Stir around to allow proper browning.

  6. When your onion becomes translucent and your protein is cooked to your liking, crack three eggs into the pan. Season with cajun seasoning to liking. You can choose to scramble them or leave them be for an over easy cook. Allow to cook for 1-2 minutes.

  7. Serve on plate and garnish with green onion.

  8. Enjoy!
